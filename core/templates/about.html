About This Project

This Hospital Management System is designed to streamline the patient registration and record management process. It allows staff to register new patients, manage their details, and view records quickly and efficiently through a user-friendly interface.

The system focuses on simplicity, responsiveness, and functionality — helping healthcare providers deliver better service by organizing patient data in one place.
